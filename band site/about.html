About The Breslauers

The Breslauers are a high-energy rockabilly band blending classic 50s, 60s and 70s vibes with a modern twist, incorporating blues, punk, rock, and much more. Founded in 2020, the band has been igniting stages with their raw sound and rebellious spirit.

Band members:

  • Lead Vocals: Karissa Hultgren
  • Bass: Ali Bozkurt
  • Guitar: Mateusz Kowalczyk
  • Drums: Chaitanya Gosavi
